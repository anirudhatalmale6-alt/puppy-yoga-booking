About Us

Where yoga meets
puppy therapy

Welcome to Puppy Flow Yoga — a one-of-a-kind yoga experience at Flex Space in Richmond Hill that combines the calming benefits of yoga with the pure joy of playing with adorable puppies.

Our classes are designed for all levels. Whether you're a seasoned yogi or stepping on a mat for the first time, our certified instructors will guide you through a gentle flow while rescue puppies roam freely, offering cuddles, kisses, and the occasional playful interruption.

Every puppy at our studio is from a local rescue. Many have found their forever homes through our classes — so you might just leave with more than inner peace.

Late Arrival & No-Show Policy

Please Arrive Early
We recommend arriving 10 minutes before your scheduled class to allow time for parking, check-in, and settling in.

10-Minute Grace Period
Attendees may enter up to 10 minutes after the class start time. Entry is not permitted beyond the 10-minute window for the safety and comfort of all participants and puppies.

No-Show Policy
Arriving after the grace period results in a no-show status with no refunds or reschedules. Late arrivals are disruptive to the class flow and puppy behavior.

Emergencies
If you have an emergency, please contact us as soon as possible before your scheduled class and we will do our best to accommodate you.

FAQs

Got questions?

What is Puppy Flow Yoga?

Puppy Flow Yoga is a premium puppy yoga and wellness experience in Richmond Hill. We merge the transformative benefits of yoga with the joy of playful, affectionate puppies, offering a deeply therapeutic and rejuvenating session.

What happens during a Wellness Puppy Yoga class?

Each session is a guided, all-levels yoga flow interwoven with delightful moments of interaction with our puppies. The class is designed to promote mindfulness, stress relief, and pure joy. Expect light stretching, gentle poses, and plenty of time to cuddle, play, and bond with our pups.

Do you need yoga experience to attend?

Our classes are designed to be beginner-friendly and accessible to everyone. We practice a gentle flow yoga style, with options and guidance throughout so you can move at your own pace and comfort level. You're always welcome to take breaks, modify poses, or simply relax on your mat — especially if you'd rather spend some time cuddling or playing with the puppies. The goal is for you to feel comfortable, supported, and have a fun, memorable experience.

Do I need to bring a mat?

No need to bring a yoga mat — we supply them for you during the class. All mats are sanitized between sessions for the health and safety of the pups and all attendees. All you need to bring is yourself, clothes that are easy to move in, and a water bottle if you want one!

What breeds will be at my class?

Breeds vary each week! Check our Breed Reveal section to see which puppies are scheduled for the upcoming weekend. We update it every week.

Where are you located?

We're at Flex Space, 10225 Yonge St Unit R, Richmond Hill, ON L4C 3B2. Free parking is available on-site. We're easily accessible from Yonge Street.

Can pregnant women participate?

Our classes are all flow yoga, which is a style of yoga that is generally considered safe for pregnant women. However, like with all new exercises during pregnancy, it's recommended that you get the go-ahead from your healthcare professional before joining a class.

What is the age requirement for attending?

For the safety and comfort of our puppies, guests, and staff, the following age policy applies to all classes:

Ages 18+ may attend alone.
Guests ages 10-17 must be accompanied by an adult (18+) who is also attending the class.
Maximum 3 minors (ages 10-17) per adult.

Every guest must have their own valid ticket. Valid photo ID will be requested at check-in to verify age, and waivers must be completed prior to participation. Guests who do not meet the age requirement or arrive without the required supervision will not be permitted to participate, and tickets will be considered forfeited.

Why does Puppy Flow Yoga have an age policy?

Our policy helps ensure a safe, comfortable experience for guests while protecting the wellbeing of the puppies. This age requirement is set in collaboration with our breeders and rescue partners and is strictly enforced to prioritize the well-being of our puppies.

Does every person attending the class need a ticket?

Yes. All attendees, including supervising adults, must purchase a ticket.

Can one adult accompany more than three minors?

No. For supervision and safety reasons, the maximum is three minors (ages 10-17) per adult (18+).

Do children need ID to be permitted entrance?

Yes. All guests must be able to show ID (or a photo of ID) that includes their date of birth.

What happens if we don't meet the requirements at arrival?

If age, supervision, ID, or waiver requirements are not met, entry may be denied.

Can I get a refund if we don't meet the age requirements?

Tickets are non-refundable if entry is denied due to not meeting age, supervision, ID, or waiver requirements. However, tickets may be rescheduled or transferred according to our standard Returns & Transfers policy.

What's your cancellation/refund policy?

All bookings are final — we do not offer refunds. However, you can reschedule to a different date/time ($7 CAD reschedule fee) or transfer your ticket to someone else for free. Store credit is also available.

Can I reschedule my booking?

Yes! You can reschedule to any available session for a $7 CAD fee per ticket. If Puppy Flow Yoga initiates the reschedule (e.g., class cancellation), no fee applies. You can also transfer your ticket to another person at no cost.

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled class time to allow for check-in and settling in. We have a strict 10-minute grace period — entry is not permitted after that for the safety and comfort of all participants.

Can I book a private event or party?

Yes! We offer private puppy yoga sessions for birthdays, bachelorette parties, corporate team-building, and other special events. Contact us through the form below or email us for details and pricing.

Are the puppies treated well?

Absolutely. The welfare of our puppies is our top priority. Sessions are carefully managed to avoid overstimulation, and puppies always have access to water, rest areas, and supervision. We work exclusively with reputable local rescues and breeders who follow ethical practices.
