About Us

Where yoga meets
puppy therapy

Welcome to Puppy Flow Yoga — a one-of-a-kind yoga experience at Flex Space in Richmond Hill that combines the calming benefits of yoga with the pure joy of playing with adorable puppies.

Our classes are designed for all levels. Whether you're a seasoned yogi or stepping on a mat for the first time, our certified instructors will guide you through a gentle flow while rescue puppies roam freely, offering cuddles, kisses, and the occasional playful interruption.

Every puppy at our studio is from a local rescue. Many have found their forever homes through our classes — so you might just leave with more than inner peace.

FAQs

Got questions?

Do I need yoga experience?

Not at all! Our classes are beginner-friendly. The instructor guides you through gentle poses at a relaxed pace. The puppies don't judge your form — we promise.

What should I bring?

Just yourself and a smile! We provide yoga mats and all equipment. Wear comfortable clothes you don't mind getting a little puppy fur on.

Are the puppies available for adoption?

Yes! All our puppies come from local rescue organizations. If you fall in love during class (and many do), we can connect you with the rescue to start the adoption process.

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 24 hours before class. Within 24 hours, we offer a credit toward a future session. No-shows are non-refundable.

How many puppies will be there?

We typically have 6-8 puppies per session, ensuring everyone gets plenty of puppy time! The breed and age may vary each week.

What if I'm allergic?

We love that you're still interested! Unfortunately, with puppies roaming freely, we can't guarantee an allergen-free environment. Please consult your doctor before booking.

Can I take photos during class?

Absolutely! We encourage it. There will be a dedicated photo break during class so you can capture those adorable moments. Just keep phones on silent during the flow portions.
